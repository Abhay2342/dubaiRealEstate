We decided to give you a simple exercise to move on on the next phase, just to take a look at your coding skills. The exercise is the following:
Create a REST backend with spring boot and java (preferably in java 21+ with a postgres as DB, but you can choose by yourself as well). In this backend you have to create endpoints for:
- Uploading and parsing raw publicly available sales and rent transactions of properties in Dubai (I will send you the CSV files shortly) and storing them in the database.
- Viewing the sales and rent transactions with filters for usage, min/max price (trans_value column in the transaction CSV, annual_amount in the rents transactions) and free text search in area and project.
- Viewing average sales or rent prices per area.
Next, create the following MCP tools in your backend:
- Get available sales areas.
- Get available rents areas.
- View last transactions of an area area.
- View average sales and rents prices of a given area.
We want you to implement this backend with hexagonal architecture, I will send you a very helpful pdf by Tom Hombergs as well. You can upload your code in a public repository of your choice (either github, gitlab or bitbucket) and prepare a postman collection for your endpoints. Are you ok with all of this?
